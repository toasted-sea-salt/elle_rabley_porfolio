Problem
McGill students have no trusted, campus-native marketplace. Facebook groups are unverified and fragmented. Craigslist has no university context. Students lose hundreds per semester on textbooks they could resell.
Solution
A verified, peer-to-peer platform accessible only to McGill email holders — combining marketplace functionality with built-in campus safety features and a reputation layer.
Stage
MVP in active development. Core marketplace functionality, authentication, and Safe-Swap Zone integration underway. Targeting campus launch in 2025.
Role
Founder — product strategy, UX design (Figma), and full-stack development.
